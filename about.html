Our Machines

Moser Excavations P/L currently has three machines available. The largest proportion of our work is conducted in the Case 25 tonne excavator. The other two machines include the 1.7 tonne Sany mini-excavator and a small bob-cat.

Latest Technology

Our 25 tonne Case excavator is fitted with the state of the art Top Con GPS technology. Together with Position Partners, we are about to use relevant and real-time maps to allow precision excavation.

Our Services

At Moser Excavations we take pride in offering excavation services that are second to none. We don't just provide the machinery; we provide an experienced operator who is an expert in handling his equipment. Our owner-operator Carl is skilled, certified, and dedicated to ensuring your project is completed safely and efficiently. Examples of services we provide include: residential excavation, commercial excavation, land clearing, trenching and drainage, site preparation etc.
